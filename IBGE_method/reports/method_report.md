# IBGE Adapted Method

Generated by `.venv/bin/python manage.py three-methods`.

## Important caveat
This is not the strict national IBGE 1 km statistical-grid product; it applies the IBGE thematic scoring logic on the drone footprint. Slope is derived from the real drone DTM on a 10 m analysis grid to avoid centimeter-scale surface roughness dominating the IBGE weights.
