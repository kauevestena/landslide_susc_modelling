# IBGE High-Resolution Adapted Method

Generated by `.venv/bin/python manage.py three-methods`.

## Important caveat
This is an IBGE-compliant high-resolution adaptation, not the strict national 1 km statistical-grid product. The IBGE weights, thematic notes and class breaks are retained, but the 1 km grade is deliberately replaced by the 16 cm drone DTM grid. Drone-derived DTM and custom deep-learning LULC are used as higher-resolution substitutes where available.
