# SGB-Style Method

Generated by `.venv/bin/python manage.py three-methods`.

## Important caveat
This is a deterministic reconstruction because no landslide-scar polygon inventory intersects the drone bbox for formal ISD calibration. Terrain factors are derived from the real drone DTM on a 5 m analysis grid.
